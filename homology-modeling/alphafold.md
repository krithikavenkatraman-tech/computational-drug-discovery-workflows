# AlphaFold Protein Structures

## Overview

AlphaFold predicts protein structures using deep learning and has significantly advanced structural biology by providing highly accurate protein models.

---

## Applications

- Protein structure prediction
- Functional annotation
- Molecular docking
- Drug discovery
- Comparative structural analysis

---

## Advantages

- High prediction accuracy
- Large structural database
- Rapid protein structure access
- Useful when experimental structures are unavailable
